Wellington

Right in the beating heart of Wellington City, our new HQ has been fitted out with plenty of space to meet and collaborate, quiet places to work (shhh in the library!) and social places to relax. Wellington is constantly rated one of the world's most liveable cities - the coffee is strong, the restaurant and bar scene is second to none, and the city's rugged natural setting provides the perfect launching pad for outdoor activities on both land and water.

Pick your perfect commute - catch the train, cycle, ferry, ride the bus, or even go old school and drive your car. Flux HQ is a ten minute walk from the city's main train station, and 5 minutes from the commuter ferry on Wellington's iconic waterfront. We're a stone's throw from some of the city's best retail spots and eateries.

High Tech capital

Wellington's booming tech sector has given the city its reputation as New Zealand's innovation and digital capital. It's compact geography means people and ideas are constantly in collision, producing some of the country's most surprising, innovative and successful creative products.

Something for everyone

There are few cities in the world that offer year-round outdoor and off-road thrills and spills, alongside a pumping cultural calendar. Whether you love hurtling downhill on mountain bikes, quaffing sour beers or decking yourself out in vintage fashions, Wellington has something to float almost everyone's boat.

Melbourne

Flux's Australian office is smack-bang in the centre of Melbourne, meaning our Aussie Fluxters are right amongst the city's world-class dining scene. From speakeasy style cocktail bars to boutique coffee roasters, there's something for every palate.

We're also a short walk away from the mighty Yarra River, a great spot for some serenity - or if you're a sportsfan, check out the tennis at the Rod Laver Arena or cricket at the MCG. Melbourne's extensive tram and bus services are also right at our doorstep, making commuting a breeze.

Tech capital of Oz

Melbourne is the undisputed tech capital of Australia, officially claiming this crown in 2017. The local tech sector employs almost 100,000 people and is seen as a leader in software development and cloud technology.

Discover more about Melbourne at
www.melbourne.vic.gov.au

Birmingham

Our Birmingham base is located on Broad Street, in the heart of the city's entertainment and gastro centre. We're also just minutes away from the city's acclaimed canal system, so you can spend your lunch or a sunny afternoon taking a walk and soaking up the atmosphere.

Broad Street is also known for being the centre of Birmingham's nightlife, so you'll never be too far from the action. And if you love football (like almost all Brummies do), you'd better choose your side Aston Villa or Birmingham City!

Tech in Birmingham

Tech is forecast to be Birmingham's fastest growing sector in the next five years. With more firms looking to the city as a liveable alternative to bigger centres, Birmingham's role as a hub of digital and creative talent looks set to grow.
