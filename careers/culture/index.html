What it's like at Flux

We don't have many rules at Flux. But we know the way we act, treat each other and work together defines who we are, what we create and how we deliver for our customers.

We care about all the things, from our shiny new HQ to our fellow crew. We give a shit about every part of our platform, our clients, their customers and concerns.

Our motto, Boldly Go, sums up what it means to be a Fluxster. We find a way, whether that means going over it, under it or through it. It means throwing caution to wind when we need to, being brave and taking chances. We know we may not always get it right the first time, but who does? That would be boring.

Flux can help you get here

Moving can be stressful, even if it's across town. Moving to the other side of the world is a whole new level, but it can also be hugely exciting if you have the right support. For international job applicants, Flux can help you navigate all the boring but necessary paperwork, so you can focus on the more important aspects of shifting to New Zealand.
