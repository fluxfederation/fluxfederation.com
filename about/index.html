What is Flux?

Besides being your launchpad for energy customer innovation, Flux is a software platform that makes it easier for you to run your energy retail business end-to-end while unlocking opportunities to innovate.

Our platform was purpose built by people who run energy companies, so we know it works. Flux is a subsidiary of Meridian Energy, New Zealand's largest energy generator and its fourth-largest retailer.

We're a team of 150+ software developers, test analysts, designers, customer experience, and product people. We're the power behind the internationally award-winning energy brand Powershop.

Buying and selling energy should be a flexible, simple and lovable experience for energy customers and the people who serve them (that’s you).

Flexible

Stay ahead of your market and competitors

There's a constant drive for innovation globally. Energy markets are changing because they have to. From regulations to price changes, the modern energy customer has different expectations and preferences. We built our platform to be adaptable with various modules and functions that can be used separately or together to suit both your customers’ needs and your market. Our platform also allows you to design and create your own enticing propositions without the need for new software development and tricky tech logistics.

Simple

Manage your business from just one system

As you know, traditional energy companies juggle as many as 40 different legacy systems from multiple suppliers - an operational headache. Flux does the job of all of these systems in one secure, integrated platform. We combine the customer stuff with the operational stuff to make it easier for you to manage your business and focus on the things that matter.

Lovable

Deliver an experience that you and your customers love

Flux is built to put customers and staff first. Use our platform to deliver refreshing, delightful experiences that make managing energy fun. You can even make your own enhancements to the experience you offer without expensive, bespoke development.
