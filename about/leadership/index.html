Ari Sargent
Chief Executive Officer

ari.sargent@fluxfederation.com

Ari is founder and CEO of Flux Federation. He might dress like a washed up DJ, but over the past decade he has spearheaded the radical transformation in electricity retailing in New Zealand, Australia and the United Kingdom. An engineer with more than 25 years’ experience in the energy industry, Ari launched Powershop as a startup in 2009 after being frustrated with the industry’s legacy systems and outdated attitudes towards customers. A tech geek at heart, Ari saw a massive opportunity to use the flexibility, transparency, speed and lower cost structures offered by digital technologies to provide a fundamentally different (and better) experience for utility partners and their customers. Ari enjoys building power companies that customers love and with Flux Federation he is pursuing further partnerships to spread the love across international energy markets.

Philippa Dawe
Chief Product Officer

philippa.dawe@fluxfederation.com

Philippa has 20+ years design, branding and marketing experience in New Zealand, Australia, the United States and the United Kingdom. Philippa has been a design leader for high profile brands including Herman Miller, Pimm’s, Smirnoff, Baileys, Renault, Marks and Spencer, BNZ, Te Papa, Trilogy and NZ Olympics. She brings business strategy to life with creative execution, and a ruthless focus on the customer. As Chief Product Officer at Flux Federation, Philippa makes sure that the experiences that our products deliver work for the humans who need to use them, to drive both customer and operational outcomes.

Mark Drysdale
Chief Technology Officer

mark.drysdale@fluxfederation.com

Mark is passionate about engineering delivery and thinks coding is more akin to artistic creation than most companies realise. Mark has over 25 years’ experience in IT delivery with the majority of that working for Fortune 100 companies in Europe and America. After graduating from Waikato University, Mark went to London and worked as a Java developer for small startups where he was introduced to XP and Agile. Mark has worked for Bupa, Lloyds, Capital One and Rabobank, where he has implemented Agile delivery models at scale. Returning to New Zealand in 2017, Mark joined Westpac to help them with a major agile transformation, and went on to join the Flux team in 2018 as Chief Technology Officer.

Emma Barnes
Crew Experience Officer

emma.barnes@fluxfederation.com

Emma has a career spanning 20+ years that includes a wide range of roles from grafting tomatoes to software testing to people care. For the last decade she’s been working for tech companies caring about quality and people. Umesh Parag
Chief Operating Officer

umesh.parag@fluxfederation.com

Umesh has a career spanning 20 years in finance. Umesh spent 10 years at KPMG in Wellington, primarily in the financial advisory services team before joining the Meridian Group in 2007. He joined Flux Federation as CFO, when it was spun out of Powershop, and is currently the Chief Operating Officer. Umesh enjoys the challenges that come with driving operational and financial performance in growing businesses, and particularly in the fast moving software development environment.

Dan Lee
VP Business Development

dan.lee@fluxfederation.com

Dan is a seasoned technology entrepreneur, with over 20 years’ experience in key technology and leadership roles in wholesale and retail energy markets systems throughout Australasia, Asia and Europe. Dan has founded two successful SaaS start-up companies and managed them to successful exit. The first was acquired by Powershop, the second by NASDAQ listed Citrix. Following two years working in Silicon Valley, Dan returned to Wellington to spearhead the rollout of Powershop in the UK and establish a high performing Flux product team. Dan now takes care of Flux’s business development activities.

Jim Barrett
Principal Product Manager

jim.barrett@fluxfederation.com

Jim has served a 19-year stretch in international energy markets, initially focused on operational management of ‘front-office’ customer-facing functions and also ‘back-office’ processing teams at both Contact Energy and Powershop. Since joining Powershop, Jim took on the additional role of Head of Product, leading the development of the Powershop platform with successful roll outs in New Zealand and Australia. He then relocated to the United Kingdom to support the creation of Powershop UK and the roll out of the platform in that market. Since returning to New Zealand, Jim has set up the client success function at Flux and has now moved into the role of Principal Product Manager.
