Who is flux?

Owned by Meridian Energy, and led by CEO Ari Sargent, we're the power behind Powershop. We build the software that powers Powershop in New Zealand, Australia and the UK. We imagine, design and build software, brands and experiences for utility companies and their customers, all around the world.

Although we're a technology company, people are at the heart of everything we do. We discover new ways for customers to experience energy, and new ways for the people who work at utility companies to manage how they sell power.
